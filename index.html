about me

Hi, I’m Michael and I’m lucky enough to be an award winning professional wedding photographer. I love my job documenting the best day of a couple’s life and I’ve been fortunate enough to photograph over 250 weddings. My style of photography is ideal if you’re like me and you’re not a fan of posing for photographs. I shoot a wedding day naturally as it happens. Couples who book me want to enjoy their wedding day with their friends and family whilst I work to capture the real story of your day.
